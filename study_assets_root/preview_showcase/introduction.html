JATOS' preview feature

Usually here would be a description of your study, but since this is an JATOS example study I want to use this text to tell you more about JATOS' preview feature.

The preview feature only works with Personal Single Workers and General Single Workers - with other workers it makes no sense, since they can start a study mulitple times, or are started externally (MTurk Worker). A normal General Single or Personal Single Worker is restrictive: once a worker clicked on the link that starts the run, that's it - JATOS will not let them run the study twice. But in some cases you may want to distribute a link and let your workers preview the first component of your study (where you could e.g. describe what they will have to do, how long it will take, ask for consent, etc). Often, workers see this description and decide that they want to do the study later.

Here is were the preview feature comes in. It allows the workers to use the link and start the study as often as they want - as long as they don't move beyond the first component. If the worker moves to the second one (or any other than the first) the study run will start 'officially' and any other usage of the link will lead to an error.

To activate the preview feature with a particular worker you just have to click the checkbox 'Allow preview' during the worker's creation. Again, this works only with Personal Single and General Single Workers.

Consent

What information will we store?

We will record your answers to all questions, along with the time that it takes you to answer each question. Additionally, we will store information about whether you clicked on other tabs or programs other than the browser tab where the study is running, the time and date in which you did the study and, in case of errors, the browser version that you are using.

Cookies

We use cookies to run this study. If you start the study, we will assume that you are happy to receive all cookies from this website.

You can quit at any time

Your participation is voluntary; you are free to withdraw your participation from this study at any time. In every component will be a 'Cancel' link in the bottom-right corner. If you do not want to continue, you can click this link to cancel the study. Your data will be automatically deleted.

By beginning the study, you acknowledge that you have read this information and agree to participate in this research, with the knowledge that you are free to withdraw your participation at any time.
